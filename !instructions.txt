Polyend Tool 1.2.1 instructions of use.

Polyend Tool is an application for convenient firmware updates. Required to upgrade the following instruments: Medusa, Poly 2, Preset, Seq. Compatible with MacOS 10.14 (or higher) and Win 10 (or higher).

- In order to flash your Polyend instrument download the right firmware update hex file too.
- Use no USB hubs or adapters, only straight connection between the instrument and the computer.
- Disabling the Windows Defender/custom AV apps for the moment of flashing may be needed.
- In case of any questions use help.polyend.com/submit_ticket to contact us.
